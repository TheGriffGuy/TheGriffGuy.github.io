how to make crafting table in minecraft in 10 easy steps

1: first step open minecraft and click play in a survival world

2: next, locate a tree (big thing with green and brown)

3: after that, walk up to the tree and say hello!

4: hold down left click and break log

5: get log

6: open inventory and place log in crafting menu

7: collect wood planks from crafting output box into inventory

8: insert wood planks (4) spread out into all slots of the crafting menu in inventory (1 in each)

9: grab crafting table

10: enjoy
